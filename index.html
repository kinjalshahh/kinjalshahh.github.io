Stuff I have done

These are some of the projects I have worked on over the past couple of years.

  • Connect Four AI

    I wrote a program using object-oriented programming, which allows user to play Connect Four with the computer. The computer makes an informed decision that can beat the average player, and is primarily based on the Minimax algorithm.

  • Text-Identification

    I developed a statistical model to predict the author of a text. The program compares the writing style of authors of any chosen texts to determine which of the chosen authors is the likelier author of a third unknown text. I used dictionaries as the primary data structure and implemented object-oriented programming.

  • Tradego

    During my internship at Facebook, along with two other interns, I designed and developed an android mobile application that provides users a platform to market products they wish to sell and search for products they wish to buy.

  • Upcoming...

    I plan on building a rideshare app in the near future so allow students to share rides to grocery stores, shopping malls, or the airport!
